# 🛡️ 08 - Safety & Environmental Requirements

## 📌 Overview

This section outlines key safety, environmental, and operational considerations when working in data center environments, as well as best practices for handling sensitive systems and information.

These principles are critical for ensuring:

* Personnel safety
* Equipment protection
* System reliability
* Data security


---

## ⚠️ Physical Safety in Data Center Environments

### 🔌 Electrical Safety

* Always assume equipment is live unless verified otherwise
* Avoid contact with exposed power connections
* Use proper grounding when handling hardware
* Never overload power circuits or extension leads


---

### 🧊 Cooling & Airflow Awareness

* Maintain proper airflow (front-to-back cooling)
* Avoid blocking vents or fan intakes
* Keep rack layouts aligned with airflow design


---

### 🧰 Safe Handling of Equipment

* Use proper lifting techniques for heavy equipment
* Secure devices in racks to prevent tipping
* Use anti-static precautions (ESD protection)


---

### 🚧 Work Area Safety

* Keep cables organized to prevent trip hazards
* Maintain clean and uncluttered workspaces
* Follow access control procedures in restricted environments


---

## 🌍 Environmental Considerations

### 🌡️ Temperature & Humidity

* Ensure systems operate within recommended temperature ranges
* Avoid exposure to moisture or condensation


---

### ⚡ Power Stability

* Use UPS systems where applicable
* Avoid sudden shutdowns that may corrupt systems


---

### ♻️ Hardware Responsibility

* Dispose of electronic waste according to regulations
* Reuse or recycle equipment where possible


---

## 🔐 Handling Sensitive Systems & Data

### 🧠 Access Control

* Use least-privilege access principles
* Do not share credentials
* Lock systems when unattended


---

### 🔑 Authentication & Credentials

* Use strong passwords
* Rotate credentials where required
* Avoid storing passwords in plain text


---

### 🖥️ System Integrity

* Avoid unauthorized configuration changes
* Document all changes made to systems
* Use version control where possible


---

### 📡 Network Security Awareness

* Be aware of exposed services (e.g. SSH, web servers)
* Restrict access to trusted devices/networks
* Monitor for unusual activity


---

## 📊 Operational Best Practices

### 📝 Documentation

* Record configurations and changes
* Maintain clear network diagrams
* Keep troubleshooting logs


---

### 🔄 Change Management

* Test changes in a controlled environment
* Apply changes incrementally
* Validate system behavior after changes


---

### 🚨 Incident Awareness

* Identify and isolate faults quickly
* Avoid making multiple changes at once
* Always verify the root cause before applying fixes


---

## 🧠 Key Learning

Working with infrastructure requires not only technical skills, but also:

* Awareness of physical safety risks
* Responsibility in handling systems and data
* Discipline in following structured operational procedures
